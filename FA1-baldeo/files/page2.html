King

  • The most valuable piece in the game
  • A very vulnerable piece and can only move one tile in any direction without obstructions (tiles that are targetted by an enemy piece, non-empty tile etc)
  • The only piece that can be "checked"
  • Once the king is in check, it is forced to either: block the check, capture the piece causing the check, or simply move the king
  • Losing this piece will immediately result in a loss
