Pawn

  • The most abundant piece counting to 8 pawns per side
  • Can only move front by one tile; if the pawn have not moved yet, it may travel 2 tiles forward
  • Can only capture diagonally by one tile far
  • If a pawn moves 2 tiles forward and encounters an enemy piece on it's side, it is vulnerable to an "En Passant" capture
  • It can promote to any piece (except the king and pawn itself) if it reaches the other side of the board
