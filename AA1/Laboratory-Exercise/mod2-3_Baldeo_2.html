Starry Night by Vincent van Gogh

This image is most famous from the music Nocturnes, Op. 9 No.2. It is a piano piece composed by Fryderyk Franciszek Chopin (A famous pianist mostly due to his expressive and touching piano playing. The original image is called Starry Nights; painted by Vincent van Gogh.

This painting is believed to be an expression of the artist. Saying that the rather distorted sky depicts the state of awe, the stars expressing the dream, heaven and death.
