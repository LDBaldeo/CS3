Parasyte

The movie Parasyte is a Japanese show that represents the attack of an alien-like creature while the protagonist also being a victim of the attack but was able to manipulate and control it's inhuman capabilities.
With his abilities, he was able to travel the infested city while also protecting himself. His main objective is to seek revenge to the main reason for the infestation becuase it took his mother's life.
